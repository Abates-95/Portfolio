WELCOME TO THE JUNGLE

Animals have always been interesting to me, below is a gallery of the most interesting of all MINE haha just kidding all animals are equally awesome, hence why I have a few living under my roof check them out!

This is Greg he is a 15 year old African Grey Parrot who loves sunflower seeds and pistachios! Greg sure is a talker he likes to say hello ask where the food is and count up to 4! He also loves to dance and sing along!

This is Bruce he is a 2 year old Blue Iguana! he loves all fresh fruits and veggies! Being cold blooded and not being able to control his own body temperature Bruce spends most of his day basking in his heat lamps soaking up the rays!

This is Pizza Bone he is a 4 year old Alligator Snapping turtle! Pizza Bone loves to eat anything that moves no fishy friends for him! Alligator Snapping turtle species are estimated to be 90 Million years and can grow as old as 120 years or older and weight upwards of 250 pounds!
